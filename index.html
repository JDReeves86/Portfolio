About me

Web and software development is a far cry from nursing, but here I stand. I have spent the past 13 years serving others during their most difficult of times. Over a decade of working with the most vulnerable in a high stakes environment has helped me foster an attitude of constant growth, self reflection and seeking excellence. Spending the last 10 years in critical care, and through the covid pandemic, I have developed an ability to act quickly and think critically through stressful situations. I now look to blend the past with my future and bring the skills I have developed over the course of my nursing career with my newfound technical skills. I hope to find a job where I can effectively leverage both of these skills to enact change while continuing to expand my abilities.
